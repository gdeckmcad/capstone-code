It's Accrual World

A choose your own adventure banking game

  • About
    It’s Accrual World is focused on helping the public gain an understanding of the struggles that the underbanked and unbanked go through. Players will navigate the ins and outs of financial services through different character scenarios while building emapthy and learning how to take action to make financial services more equitable.
  • Unbanked
    Unbanked refers to adults who do not use or do not have access to any traditional financial services including savings accounts, credit cards, or personal checks
  • Underbanked
    Underbanked households often rely on cash and alternative financial services instead of on credit cards and traditional loans to fund purchases and manage their finances. Many underbanked households lack access to affordable banking and financial services.
  • Predatory Lending
    Predatory lending is any practice of a lender that imposes unfair and abusive loan terms on borrowers, including high interest rates, high fees, and terms that strip the borrower of equity.
  • Get Started
    On the next screen you will receive a random character scenario. Navigate through the character’s day to day, making choices with the financial services available. As you go take note of the call to actions for how to make financial services more equitable.
