Welcome to It’s Accrual World!

A choose your own adventure banking game where you are grounded in real character scenarios to build financial health, literacy, and resilience as you navigate real-world financial decisions through the characters’ lens.

What adventure will you choose?

Carlos is a Contractor and he just got paid for a recent job. Carlos now needs to pay his workers and get supplies for his next job. Should he cash his recent pay check or deposit in a bank?

Grace wants to be a UX designer after High School, she is looking at programs and wants to be smart about time and money. Where should she go after graduation?

Janice has saved up money to move out of their parents house. They want to make sure they are making an investment but can still afford other expenses. Should they rent or buy?
